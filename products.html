Why you should watch Cowboy Bebop

What Cowboy Bebop is all about

The anime was released in the 90's and is set in 2071, following a group of bounty hunters and their intergalactic travles as they hunt down fugitives. While on its face a bit of a ridiculous premise, the show deals with heavy topics and can cause the viewer to have a lot of introspection.

What's so good about it?

  • An episodic anime, meaning each episode has its own adventure but it ties into the broader narrative of the show
  • The show provides interesting insight as to the Japanese perception of American taste as Cowboy Bebop was one of the first animes made specifically for western consumtion
  • Very character driven show with a unique and interesting cast of characters, my favorite being the main character Spike.
  • It tackles more adult and mature concepts like delaing with loneliness and overcoming a flawed past and coming to terms with ones self.
  • Something new to see every time you watch it with various subtle hints and refences that viewers can pick up on after having wathced the anime over
  • Unique art that is influenced by various cultures
